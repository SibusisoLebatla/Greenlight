About-Us

Green Light Africa is a pioneering Social Impact Marketing Agency, operating at the intersection of social impact, creativity, and technology. Our mission is to leverage the power of strategic marketing and creative storytelling to drive societal change. We provide businesses, organisations, and brands with tailored solutions that integrate social impact into their core operations, ensuring they meet the growing demand for meaningful corporate responsibility.

In today's world, consumers are increasingly drawn to businesses that actively contribute to positive societal outcomes.With sustainability, inclusivity, and purpose as driving forces, Green Light Africa empowers entities to align their brand values with impactful areas. By doing so, they not only fulfill their corporate responsibilities but also foster deeper connections with their audiences.
Green Light Africa offers a range of specialised marketing and impact-driven services designed to help businesses become active citizens within their communities. We craft and execute social impact campaigns that not only boost a brand’s visibility but also create measurable positive change.

FOUNDER

Zimi Mabunzi's journey is one deeply rooted in service, storytelling, and a relentless passion for creating change. Hailing from Middledrift, a village in the Eastern Cape, her path was profoundly shaped by the experience of financial exclusion from university in 2014. This setback ignited a passion for service and development, driving her to dedicate the next eight years of her life working with non-profits. Through this work, she began her mission to uplift communities and build meaningful impact, especially focusing on empowering young girls and women. In 2016, Zimi was crowned Miss Eastern Cape, marking the beginning of her public journey as a role model for young people across South Africa. Her platform grew further when she became a runner-up in the Miss South Africa 2021 pageant, allowing her to expand her network and influence. These experiences not only showcased her poise and passion but also solidified her role as an advocate for social change. Throughout these years, Zimi built a name for herself, not only in the world of pageantry but as a speaker, facilitator, and storyteller, using her platform to champion the causes closest to her heart. Zimi is also leveraging her storytelling abilities across various media. Whether through captivating digital creation visuals or engaging narratives, she has used her digital presence to inspire and inform, working with reputable brands and creating content that resonates with her audience. Her experience as a storyteller is further amplified through her role as a TV host on DSTV’s Home Channel, where she anchors the show Afrimaxx. Here, she brings Africa’s stories to life—whether it's the intricate designs of local artisans, the rich flavors of African cuisine, or the continent’s vibrant history—Zimi’s passion for telling African stories is undeniable. Today, as the founder of Green Light Africa and the Green Light Africa Foundation, Zimi is taking on a new role as a Social Impact Specialist. Through these platforms, she is crafting the impact stories of businesses and organisations, helping them integrate social responsibility into their core strategies. By merging her love for storytelling with her commitment to social change, Zimi is not just telling stories—she is creating new narratives of impact, service, and sustainable development.

Our-Vision-and-Mission

Our Vision

At Green Light Africa, we believe that businesses can play a pivotal role in addressing societal challenges. We envision a world where brands are active citizens, contributing to a sustainable future while creating value for their shareholders. Our long-term goal is to build a network of businesses and changemakers working together to solve Africa’s most pressing issues—from youth unemployment to environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

The mission of Green Light Africa is to empower businesses, brands, and organisations to become active contributors to societal well-being by integrating social impact into their core strategies. Through innovative marketing solutions and strategic partnerships, we aim to create a bridge between corporate objectives and societal needs, fostering a culture of active citizenship and sustainable development. At Green Light Africa, we are committed to helping our clients make a positive difference while achieving long-term growth and success.
